Site Purpose

This is the site plan for my project website. The website is for a nail salon called "Nails To You!" The website will have four pages: Home, Booking, Contact, and Feedback. The home page will have a brief introduction to the business. The booking page will allow users to book appointments online. The contact page will have the business contact information. The feedback page will have a feedback form for customers to leave reviews.
